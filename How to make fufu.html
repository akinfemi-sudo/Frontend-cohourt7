How to Make Fufu

Fufu is a popular West African delicacy often served with soups like egusi, ogbono, and vegetable soup. It is smooth, stretchy, and delicious. This simple guide will teach you how to prepare soft, perfect fufu at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cassava flour (or fufu powder)
  • 4 cups of water
  • A wooden turning stick (omorogun)
  • A cooking pot
  • A bowl of cold water (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pour 3 cups of water into a pot and allow it to boil.
  2. Gradually add the cassava flour while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Keep turning the mixture on medium heat until it becomes thick and stretchy.
  4. Add a little water, cover the pot, and allow it to steam for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Turn the fufu again until it is smooth and well-cooked.
  6. Mold it into balls using a bowl of cold water to smooth the surface.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite soup.

Enjoy your homemade fufu!
